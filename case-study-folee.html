Integrated Media Planning & Brand Strategy

Folée — Cupcake Brand Launch

Project Overview

Folée is a hypothetical Paris-inspired cupcake brand developed as a brand strategy and integrated media planning project.

The project focused on creating a premium yet accessible dessert brand designed for urban Gen Z and Millennial consumers who value aesthetic food experiences and social media culture.

The work included brand concept development, product design, packaging strategy, and a 12-month Integrated Marketing Communication (IMC) plan combining digital marketing, print advertising, and experiential activations.

Objective

The project aimed to:

  • Develop a go-to-market strategy for a new dessert brand
  • Position Folée as an affordable luxury cupcake brand inspired by Parisian pâtisserie culture
  • Identify and target urban Gen Z and Millennial consumers (16–30)
  • Design a 12-month integrated media strategy combining digital, print, and on-ground marketing
  • Build a strong visual brand identity and packaging system

Concept & Strategy

Brand Concept & Target Market

Folée was positioned as “A Bite of Paris in Every Swirl” — a dessert brand combining French pâtisserie inspiration with modern social-media aesthetics. The brand focused on Instagram-friendly presentation, boutique gift-ready packaging, French-inspired artisanal flavours, and affordable luxury.

The brand targeted urban Gen Z and Millennial consumers aged 16–30 in cities with strong café culture (e.g., Bangalore, Chennai, Coimbatore, Kochi). Key segments included food explorers, gifting buyers, self-care seekers, and seasonal celebration shoppers.

Product & Packaging Strategy

The product line included six French-inspired flavours: Crème de Vanille, Chocolat Noir, Fraise Rosé, Citron Meringue, Pistache Parisienne, and Café Mocha.

Packaging was designed using pastel aesthetics, vintage floral elements, and eco-friendly matte cardboard to reinforce premium and gift-ready identity. Each box included boutique presentation styling, Folée branding rings, and recycling indicators.

Integrated Media & Experiential Strategy

The marketing strategy combined multiple channels to maximize visibility:

  • Digital Marketing: Instagram Reels, aesthetic dessert content, influencer reviews, Google Ads, SEO, and a 'Cupcake Club' loyalty program.
  • Print Media: Features in lifestyle magazines such as FEMINA, Grazia, Cosmopolitan India, MetroPlus, and Times Life.
  • Out-of-Home (OOH): Mall standees, Café QR posters, and College festival booths.
  • Experiential Marketing: Campus and café sampling, pop-up kiosks in malls, QR feedback, and influencer mystery boxes.

12-Month Media Planning

A year-long campaign calendar maintained consistent brand engagement. Key themes included a Launch Campaign (#FoleeFirstBite), Valentine’s gifting, Spring flavour drops, Summer Mango Fleur launch, Paris Café Collection, Diwali boxes, and Holiday bundles.

Key Outcome

The project resulted in a complete brand launch framework for a dessert startup, including brand positioning strategy, product concept, packaging design, an IMC plan, and a 12-month media calendar. The strategy demonstrated how a dessert brand can differentiate itself through visual storytelling, seasonal campaigns, and experiential marketing targeting young urban consumers.
